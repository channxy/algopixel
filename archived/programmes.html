Scratch Junior

Introduction to the fundamentals of coding through fun and interactive activities. Scratch junior is designed for young children to create stories, games, and animations by snapping together colorful blocks, fostering creativity and problem-solving skills in an age-appropriate environment.

Game Development | 4-6 years old

Scratch

Scratch is a visual, block-based coding language that enables kids to bring their ideas to life through animations, games, and interactive stories, all while honing their coding and problem-solving skills

Game Development | 7-9 years old

Thunkable

Thunkable's user-friendly platform empowers young coders to create mobile apps for Android and iOS devices without extensive coding knowledge. With a drag-and-drop interface and visual blocks, kids design interactive apps, from games to utilities. Thunkable unlocks their creativity, making app development accessible, engaging, and fun. This track nurtures problem-solving, critical thinking, and digital innovation, setting the stage for young app developers to shine.

App Development | 10-12 years old

Web Development Fundamentals

Web Development | 10-18 years old
